Finding Harmony: Screen Time & Real Life Elements

Understanding Digital Wellbeing

Digital wellbeing isn't about giving up your phone or quitting gaming forever. It’s about feeling in control of the technology you use, instead of letting your technology control you. It means using devices intentionally so they don't harm your mental health, sleep cycles, or offline friendships.

Healthy Screen Habits
  • ✓ Setting structured, automated runtime thresholds on scrolling applications.
  • ✓ Relocating physical charging infrastructure outside the personal bedtime zone.
  • ✓ Muting secondary utility channel notifications during high-intensity revision intervals.
Unhealthy Habits & Signals
  • ✕ Engaging in continuous data scrolling loops late into recovery periods.
  • ✕ Experiencing sudden irritability when devices fall out of communication proximity.
  • ✕ Bypassing interpersonal encounters or assignments to run an extra competitive match.
